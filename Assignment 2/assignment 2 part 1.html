About Platypuses:

Platypuses are deffinetly the weirdest animals to ever walk the earth.
Their scientific name is Ornithorhynchus anatinus, and they are one of 2 monotremes (mammals that lay eggs), the other one being Echidnas. Platypusses are mammals, with the body of a beaver, the bill of a duck, and the males have venomous spurs on their back hind legs. When the puggles first hatch, they all are born with these venomus spirs. After 3 weeks, the venomus spirs fall off if they are a female and stay if male.
A platypuses diet consistd of cray fish, worms, and shrimp. When they are underwater, they close their eyes, ears and nose, they then use electrosensors in their bills to find food. When they find it, they use rocks to gerind up the food. Intrestingly, platypuses dont have stomachs, so they digest their food in their intestines. Something more known is that platypuses don't have nipples, instead they sweat milk through their skin, and also glow a teal colour under UV lights.
